About RCC WMS

The Rajshahi City Corporation Waste Management System is a smart civic dashboard for real-time monitoring of secondary transfer stations (STS) across Rajshahi city. It enables citizens to stay informed, report issues, and hold the corporation accountable — while giving administrators the tools to track, respond, and improve service delivery.

The system tracks fill levels dynamically based on clearance schedules, records historical data every 10 minutes, and lets citizens file complaints that are tracked all the way through to resolution.

📊 Live Station MonitoringReal-time fill levels, countdown timers, and clearance status for all STS locations.
🗺️ GPS Map ViewInteractive Leaflet map with colour-coded station pins based on current fill level.
📈 Station HistoryFill level charts over 7, 14, or 30 days per station — powered by live backend data.
⚠️ Complaint SystemCitizens can file complaints per station (overflow, odour, damage, etc.) with status tracking.
👤 Citizen LoginRegister and log in to track your own complaint history and receive status updates.
🛡️ Admin DashboardAnalytics, complaint management, user control, and monthly PDF report generation.
📄 PDF ReportsOne-click monthly reports with station summaries, complaint breakdowns, and trends.
